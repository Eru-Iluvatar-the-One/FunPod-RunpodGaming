# Total War: Three Kingdoms — Definitive Mod Loadout
# Generated: 2026-03-28 by Station IV
# For FunPod gaming sessions

## TIER 1: ESSENTIAL (Install First, This Order)

### 1. 190 Expanded [Overhaul] by Ironic
- **What:** Complete campaign overhaul — new regions, mechanics, balance
- **Source:** Steam Workshop
- **Load:** Core framework, load first after its prerequisites

### 2. 190 Expanded [Factions & Characters] by Ironic
- **What:** Companion to Overhaul — new playable factions, unique characters
- **Requires:** 190 Expanded Overhaul

### 3. 190 Expanded's Extra Regions by Ironic
- **What:** Additional map regions for expanded campaign
- **Requires:** 190 Expanded Overhaul

### 4. The Gathering: Core Object 1.2.2 by Inter-object
- **What:** Core framework for multiple mods — load before character mods

### 5. Make Them Unique (MTU) + Unique Portraits (TUP)
- **What:** Unique models, portraits, skills, items for 100+ heroes who lack them in vanilla
- **Why:** The #1 most recommended mod across all sources
- **Author:** Drunken_PANDA
- **Source:** Steam Workshop

### 6. Disable Looters by JX83
- **What:** Removes the universally-hated looter army mechanic
- **Why:** Every mod guide lists this as mandatory

## TIER 2: VISUAL + IMMERSION

### 7. Realistic Battle Blood by kelstr
- **What:** Better blood effects in combat
- **Source:** Nexus Mods

### 8. Vanilla Units Remaster by Deema
- **What:** Reskins units for more variety — new bodyguards, ornamental weapons, tier-appropriate gear
- **Source:** Steam Workshop

### 9. Historical Weapon Model Pack by harrylee95
- **What:** Historically accurate weapon models — spears, halberds, swords, helmets
- **Source:** Steam Workshop

### 10. Enhanced Battle Camera
- **What:** Extended zoom range, cinematic camera options, UI toggle for immersion
- **Source:** Steam Workshop

### 11. Wu Kingdaissance
- **What:** Major character reskins — new weapons, armor, mounts, abilities for Wu faction characters

### 12. Realistic Visuals Overhaul
- **What:** Removes haze, better color/lighting, zero performance impact
- **Source:** Nexus Mods (top rated)

## TIER 3: GAMEPLAY TWEAKS

### 13. AI No Execute
- **What:** Prevents AI from executing captured characters (huge QoL)

### 14. Reduced General Death Chance
- **What:** Legendary generals die less often — keeps your roster stable

### 15. Extended Military Forge Options
- **What:** More building chains beyond the vanilla 3 options

## ALTERNATIVE: RADIOUS OVERHAUL
If 190 Expanded doesn't click, Radious is the other major overhaul:
- Radious Total War Mod (two parts)
- Compatible with MTU/TUP
- New units, campaign mechanics, battle balance, scripting
- Has extensive sub-mod ecosystem
